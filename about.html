About Me

I’m a passionate and interdisciplinary problem-solver, currently working toward my B.S. in Computer Science at Oregon State University, with a foundation in Anthropology (B.A. from University of Los Angeles California). My unique combination of ethnographic fieldwork experience and technical expertise allows me to approach design challenges from a holistic, user-centered perspective.

Throughout my studies, I’ve developed a strong understanding of both qualitative and quantitative research methods, including data collection and analysis. This background enables me to draw insights that drive informed, empathetic design decisions. I’m dedicated to creating intuitive, engaging, and meaningful user experiences that not only meet business goals but also resonate with users on a deeper level.

As I continue to grow in the field of UX, my goal is to positively contribute to a company’s success by leveraging my skills in research, design, and problem-solving to build digital products that are both effective and user-friendly.
